Atmel ATmega and ISA VGA

Interested in easy to use VGA solution for embedded applications? Click here!

This project connects ISA VGA card to Atmel AVR ATmega 128.

Supported cards

The following cards are supported:
  • Realtek RTG3105iEH
  • Trident TVGA9000i
  • Trident TVGA9000B
  • Trident TVGA8900CL
  • Cirrus GD5401
  • Cirrus GD5402
  • Oak OTI077
